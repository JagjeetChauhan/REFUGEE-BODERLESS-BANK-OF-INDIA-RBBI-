Refugee Situation in India

A. Overview of Refugee Population in India
     1. Challenges Faced by Refugees:
         a: Limited Access to Financial Services: A substantial portion of refugees lacks access to traditional banking, hindering their financial inclusion.
        b: Identity Crisis: Many refugees face challenges in establishing a legal identity, which further exacerbates their financial struggles.

B. Governmental Efforts and Challenges:
     1. overnment Support:
         a: While the Indian government has made efforts to support refugees, challenges persist in providing comprehensive financial assistance and independence.

    2. Current Financial Independence Scenario:
         a: A considerable percentage of the refugee population faces difficulties in securing stable employment and financial independence.
        b: Traditional banking systems often pose challenges for refugees due to documentation issues and identity verification hurdles.

How Our Extension Addresses the Crisis

A. Impactful Intervention
     1. Financial Inclusion:
         a: By leveraging blockchain and a prepaid MasterCard, our extension directly addresses the financial inclusion gap faced by refugees in India.

    2. Streamlined Identity Verification:
         a: The decentralized identity system ensures a secure and streamlined process, eliminating barriers refugees face in establishing their identity.

B. Revolutionizing Financial Independence
     1. Quantifiable Impact:
         a: With transparent transactions and secure digital identities, refugees gain the tools needed for economic empowerment.

    2. Government Collaboration:
         a: The extension is designed to complement existing government initiatives, making financial support more efficient and targeted.

C. Addressing Systemic Failures
     1. Government Subsidy Disbursement:
         a: Our blockchain-based system aims to significantly reduce this percentage, ensuring timely and transparent subsidy distribution.

    2. Reducing Dependency:
         a: The extension aspires to reduce the dependency of refugees on external aid, fostering self-sufficiency and resilience.
